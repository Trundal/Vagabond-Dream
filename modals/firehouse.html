Where it all began

This was my first full-time job in the film industry. I had just graduated from my Animation course and was fortunate to find a position so soon thereafter. I worked here from 2001-2005.

Due to my previous university studies in computer science, my first responsibility was as the 'Interactive Media Developer' (IE: DVD & CD-ROM Authoring and Web Development). Over time I took over as the primary animation/motion-graphics expert and offline editor.
