So cute, so technically complex

Dukiboo Estonia is a designer and seller of ethnic children's clothing, providing ages-old traditional-inspired patterns using modern printing techniques and comfortable fabrics at very resonable prices.

In support of the Dukiboo startup effort, we at Vagabond Dream provided photographic services for the early marketing efforts, IT support services to keep the long-loved computers running, and an incredibly affordable webstore to sell products to their adoring fans. The Dukiboo story serves as a prime example of the "whatever it takes, whatever is needed" approach of providing flexible solutions to complex needs.
