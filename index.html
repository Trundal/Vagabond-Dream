About

  • Korey Powell

    A wilderness child from the Great White North, Korey has cheerfully wandered this big, beautiful world for most of his adult life. A playful vagabond at heart, he's learned that all the world's peoples are as extraordinary as any other.

    It's no surprise, then, that he should embrace the storyteller's art. Be it film, photography or prose, whatever the medium what matters most is the expression of the heart.

    As any good Alaskan will tell you, survival is not an accident; it's the result of careful preparation. In this digital age, one must not only be equipped with sufficient computing power - but also highly skilled in its use.
